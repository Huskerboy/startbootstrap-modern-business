Barnes Pumps

One of the oldest submersible pump lines on the market offers a wide range of sump, effluent, sewage ejectors, fountain, grinder and specialty pumps.

RRR stocks over 50 different Barnes pumps at all times ranging from a 1/3 hp sump pump to a 5hp sewage ejector and everything in between.
