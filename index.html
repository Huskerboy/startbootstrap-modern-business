LeakBreaker Water Heater Shut-Off

The Taco LeakBreaker is a water heater leak protection device that protects again flood damage from the containment failure of a water heater. It consists of a control unit, valve with actuator and snesor. This resettable, testable protection device can be installed on any style water heater to shut-off the incoming water supply, preventing additional water flow to a damaged water heater. The two color LED light and audible alarm lets you know the status of the LeakBreaker at all times.
