About RRR Industrial Sales

Serving commercial and residential plumbers with high quality pumping equipment since 1995

RRR Industrial Sales, L.P. is a family owned and operated Pump Distributor located in Dallas, Texas. Our business is centered around our knowledgeable employees and their industry leading expertise about all things pumps. We built our reputation by handling the highest quality products in order to support our customers. We provide our customers with industry leading technical support and customer service.
