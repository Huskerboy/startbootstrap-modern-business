About RRR Industrial Sales

Serving Texas and Oklahoma with high quality pumping equipment for over 20 years

RRR Industrial Sales, L.P. is a family owned and operated Pump Distributor located in Dallas, Texas since 1996. Our business has grown because of our exceptional employees who are knowledgeable about our products and markets. We have built a reputation for handling thehighest quality products to support the Plumbing Wholesale residentail and commercial markets. We believe in supporting the Plumbing Wholesaler with technical support, customer service and inventory, so they can better support their customers.
