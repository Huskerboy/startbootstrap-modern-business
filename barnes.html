Barnes Pressure Sewer Systems

Barnes Pressure Sewer Systems are a practical, environmentally safe alternative to gravity sewers. Pressure Sewer products are ideal in locations with difficult terrain as they reduce sewage infrastructure cost by allowing smaller diameter piping to be used in place of large complex sewage systems.
